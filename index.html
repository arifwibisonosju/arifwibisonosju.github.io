About Me

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at Sejong University, Seoul, Republic of Korea, majoring in Intelligent Mechatronics Engineering and Convergence Engineering for Intelligent Drone.

My passion lies in autonomous systems and intelligent robotics, with research spanning multi-agent reinforcement learning, underwater robotics, and intelligent drone technologies.

Lecturer

I previously served as a lecturer at Universitas PGRI Semarang, Indonesia, where I taught courses in computer programming, graphic design, and information technology. In addition to teaching, I mentored students in robotics, and collaborative projects. Currently, I am on study leave to pursue my Ph.D.
